Instructions

  • Select the desired level of difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard.Click on the "Start" button to begin playing.
  • You will see a 9x9 grid divided into nine 3x3 boxes.Some of the cells in the grid will be pre-filled with numbers.Your goal is to fill in the remaining empty cells with numbers from 1 to 9. Each row, column, and 3x3 box must contain all the numbers from 1 to 9, without repetition.
  • No two cells in the same row, column, or 3x3 box can have the same number.To select a cell, simply click on it.You can use the keyboard's number keys to enter the numbers into the selected cell.
  • If all the numbers are correctly placed, you win the game.If you want to start a new game, simply click on the "End Game" button.
  • You have 3 lifes i.e you loose a life for every wrong entry.
