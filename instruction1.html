Instructions

  • Before starting the game enter the number of moves you want to complete the game in.
  • The minimum number of moves required to flip all the cards are 8,flipping a pair of cards is considered as one move.
  • The game starts with all the cards face down and player takes turns to turn over two cards.
  • If the two cards match, then they remain intact, or else they flip back.
  • The time limit for the game is 180 seconds.
  • The game concludes upon the successful pairing of all cards, the completion of the designated time limit, or the exhaustion of the alloted number of moves.
